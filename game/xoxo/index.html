How to Play:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid
  • Player X goes first, then players alternate turns
  • Place your symbol in an empty cell on your turn
  • The first player to get 3 of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all cells are filled with no winner, the game is a draw
